Upcoming & Current Litters

We raise only a couple of carefully planned litters each year. All puppies are health-checked, micro-chipped, vaccinated, and come with an EU pet passport and a lifetime of breeder support.

The aim of our breeding are healthy, guidable, exhibition-show and family dogs. We mate our females with strong selected males who achieve best results on shows, have good pedigree and the right character. We organize obedience-training courses specifically designed for our puppies.
