About Me

My journey began in 1980 with a German-Shepherd female named Biba. Guided only by training manuals, Biba and I mastered every obedience exercise. In 1989 I welcomed a pedigreed male, Lux II Golobov; together we earned SchH II through obedience, tracking and protection work while enjoying the show circuit. From Lux’s litter I kept his daughter Nella Golobova; with her I reached the sport’s summit, SchH III, and competed successfully in working-dog trials.

Seeking a fresh challenge and a truly distinctive temperament, I turned to the Siberian Husky. My foundation female, Dadra Ojimi (“Dedra”), opened a new era in both training and breeding. Dedra—and the two outstanding females that followed—achieved the highest obedience titles, proving that with dedication, knowledge and passion, Huskies can excel far beyond the show ring.

Today, as a certified Master Trainer, I practise ethical breeding, positive-reinforcement methods and an unwavering commitment to giving every puppy the very best start in life.
